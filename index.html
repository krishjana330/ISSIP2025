About the Conference

The International Conference on Complex System Modelling and Artificial Intelligence (CSMAI 2026) aims to bring together researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to discuss the latest advancements, trends, and challenges in the fields of complex systems and artificial intelligence.

This conference serves as a global platform for the exchange of innovative ideas, emerging methodologies, and transformative research that bridge theoretical foundations with real-world applications. Participants will explore cutting-edge developments in AI-driven system modeling, computational intelligence, data analytics, cyber-physical systems, and intelligent automation.

About UAMD

Universiteti “Aleksandër Moisiu” Durrës (UAMD) is one of Albania’s leading public universities, known for its commitment to academic excellence, innovation, and international collaboration.

Situated in the coastal city of Durrës — a vibrant hub of culture, technology, and commerce — UAMD provides an inspiring environment for learning, research, and innovation, aligning with its mission to contribute to regional and global development through education and knowledge.

Call for Papers

Researchers, academicians, and practitioners are invited to submit original and unpublished research papers.

Complex System Modelling

  • Nonlinear dynamic systems
  • Computational modeling and simulation
  • Multi-agent systems
  • Network theory and analysis
  • Self-organization and emergent behavior

AI and Machine Learning

  • Deep learning and neural networks
  • Reinforcement learning and optimization
  • Explainable AI (XAI) and trustworthy AI
  • Cognitive computing and hybrid intelligence
  • Natural language processing and computer vision

Applications of AI & Complex Systems
