About UAMD

A Hub of Innovation

Universiteti “Aleksandër Moisiu” Durrës (UAMD) is one of Albania’s leading public universities, known for its commitment to academic excellence, innovation, and international collaboration. Established to meet the growing demands of modern education, UAMD focuses on developing future-ready graduates equipped with critical thinking, digital competence, and global awareness.

Situated in the coastal city of Durrës — a vibrant hub of culture, technology, and commerce — UAMD provides an inspiring environment for learning, research, and innovation.

A Memorable Venue

Experience the history and beauty of Durrës, Albania.

The Coastal City of Durrës

As one of the oldest cities in the Mediterranean, Durrës offers a unique blend of ancient history and modern vibrancy. Participants can explore Roman amphitheaters, Byzantine forums, and Venetian towers, all while enjoying the beautiful Adriatic coastline.

Vibrant Culture & Scenery

Beyond the conference, enjoy the local culture, cuisine, and stunning seaside views. The city is a hub of activity, providing an inspiring backdrop for innovation, collaboration, and networking.
